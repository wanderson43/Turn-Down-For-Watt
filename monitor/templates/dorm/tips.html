ELECTRICITY TIPS

  • Turn off all lights, appliances and electronics not in use.
  • Unplug the Power Strip.
  • Unplug Large Appliances.
  • Turn your air conditioner off.

WATER TIPS

  • Wash clothes in cold water.
  • Shorter Showers.
  • Bring re-usable water bottles.
  • Pre-rinse dishes.
